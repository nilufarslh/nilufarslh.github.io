Hi! I'm Farhad, a PhD student in Economics at Simon Fraser University (SFU). My research focuses on search and match models, econometrics, and macroeconomic modeling.

I am passionate about understanding dynamic economic environments and developing mathematical models to explain labor markets, financial systems, and economic policy.

Previously, I completed my master's in economics at Sharif University and have experience in statistical programming with R, Python, MATLAB, and Stata.

Current Research Interests:

  • Search and matching theory
  • Time-varying econometrics
  • Macroeconomic modeling
  • Instrumental variable estimation
